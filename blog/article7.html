Perks

A New Layer of Challenge

As you have all been waiting for, Team 12 would like to present a new update that will advance the gameplay of CharacterConnect. As I'm sure some of you may have already guessed, we are now introducing perks into the game, for all game modes.

Perks were inspired to bring about new ways to play the game. Now with proper strategic thinking, players can completely reverse the situation of a match, adding towards building a competitive environment for players to enjoy and challenge themselves.

The current perks are: 'Free Letter', 'Two Random Letters', and 'Change Bag'.

We don't want to spoil the fun by divulging into what each perk does, so we hope our players can enjoy through interacting with them. That being said, we hope to include additional perks in the future, so any feedback is appreciated on your current thoughts regarding perks.
